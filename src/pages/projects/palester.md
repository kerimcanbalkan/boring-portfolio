Palester is a modern fitness progress tracking application that enables users to log and monitor their workout performance. Users can select their workout days and specific programs for each week day, and after each session, they record the weights used for each exercise. The app features a calendar view where workout days are highlighted in green, rest days in dark green, and missed sessions in red. This intuitive system allows users to easily track their weight progression and workout consistency over time, offering a clear and engaging overview of their fitness journey.
